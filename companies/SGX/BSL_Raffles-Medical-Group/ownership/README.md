# Insider ownership dataset

This directory models reported insider interests as a dated dependency graph.
It is designed for later ownership charts and review signals without adding
overlapping deemed interests more than once.

## Files

- `nodes.csv` is the stable master of people, entities, and the issuer.
- `edges.csv` records direct-holding, deemed-interest, and family/control
  relationships. `holding_component_id` is the de-duplication key.
- `component-observations.csv` records the latest separately identifiable share
  blocks. A component may appear on several deemed-interest edges but is counted
  once in a de-duplicated total.
- `reported-interests.csv` preserves each filer's reported total at a date. It
  does not silently equate the annual-report household column with Form 1 direct
  interest.
- `disclosure-events.csv` records dated changes from SGX Form 1 filings.
- `groups.csv` defines the ownership series that may be used as signals.
- `signals.csv` and `group-series.csv` are generated by
  `../models/insider_ownership.py`.

## Interpretation contract

**Reported fact:** A deemed interest is a disclosure attribution. It can arise
through a controlled company, spouse, or another disclosed relationship. It is
not necessarily a share block registered in the filer's own name.

**Calculated metric:** A group total may only add unique
`holding_component_id` values. For example, the 62,755,366 shares held through
S & D Holdings are attributed to Dr Loo, Dr Sarah Lu, and Dr David Lu but count
once in any de-duplicated total.

The primary series is `group_loo_disclosed`: Dr Loo's own Form 1 total interest.
It is the cleanest control signal because it is a stable regulatory filer view
and already consolidates his direct and deemed interests.

Any reduction produces `reduction_review`; it is a prompt to inspect the filing,
not a sell decision. Before interpreting a reduction, classify whether it came
from a market sale, internal transfer, option exercise, estate planning, a
change in group membership, or a change in the voting-share denominator.

The current-board de-duplicated group is defined for future use but should not be
charted as a continuous signal until dated component observations and director
join/leave effects are complete. A director retirement is a membership change,
not an insider sale.

## Units and dates

- Dates use ISO `YYYY-MM-DD`.
- Share counts are ordinary voting shares.
- Ownership percentages are stored as decimals (`0.56313` = `56.313%`).
- Transaction consideration is SGD per share.
