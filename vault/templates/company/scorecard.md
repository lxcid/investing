## Assessment anchors

- **Strong:** evidence shows high quality on this dimension under
  sector-appropriate criteria.
- **Adequate:** evidence shows an acceptable baseline, but with mixed features
  or no clear advantage.
- **Weak:** evidence shows a material deficiency or risk on this dimension.
- **Unknown:** evidence is insufficient, stale, or contradictory.

Assess the company dimension independently of whether it supports the current
thesis. Describe thesis contribution separately in `thesis.md`.

Confidence is `Low`, `Medium`, or `High` based on the quantity, quality, and
recency of evidence. Catalysts may be described in the thesis when relevant but
are not required or scored.
